Kyrie Irving's Top Career Plays

1. 2016 Game Winning Shot Against Warriors

Kyrie Irving won the game against the Golden State Warriors on December 25, 2016, when he scored 42 points and made a crucial three-pointer to secure a 111-107 victory. This game was part of the 2016 NBA Finals, where the Cleveland Cavaliers faced the Golden State Warriors. Irving's performance was pivotal in leading the Cavaliers to their first NBA championship title.

2. 2025 Buzzer Beater Left Hand on Jokic

Kyrie Irving's game-winning shot against the Denver Nuggets was a remarkable display of skill and determination. With the game tied at 105-105, Irving received the inbound pass from the sidelines and, despite being chased by Nuggets star Nikola Jokic, executed a left-handed hook shot from just over 20 feet away. This shot not only secured the victory for the Dallas Mavericks but also showcased Irving's ability to make clutch plays under pressure. Jokic, who was committed to defending Irving, praised the shot as impressive, highlighting Irving's talent and the Mavericks' ability to trust him with critical moments. The game-winning shot was a testament to Irving's status as a top-tier player and his ability to perform under the most challenging circumstances.
